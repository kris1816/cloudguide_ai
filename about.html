Seamless Visitor Experience

Once your audioguide is uploaded to your CloudGuide profile, it becomes instantly accessible through our mobile applications. The platform supports over 1000 destinations worldwide, with 68 sites already utilizing our AI-generated content.

Visitors can access guides through traditional menu selection or our revolutionary camera recognition feature. When they point their phone at any exhibit or landmark, the relevant audioguide content starts playing automatically.

The integration between content generation and delivery ensures that updates made through the generator are immediately reflected in the visitor experience.
